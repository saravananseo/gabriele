The Artist

An international
icon of realism.

Gabriele Pellerone is an Italian tattoo artist and visual artist, active between Milan and Reggio Calabria, with a strong presence in New York City. He began drawing at the age of three and approached tattooing as a self-taught artist at twenty, transforming his artistic vision into a unique language that blends skin, art, and Web 3.0.

Specializing in realistic and portrait tattoos, he is followed by over 1.5 million people online. He has tattooed Travis Scott, Central Cee, Cash XO, and Baby Gang, and was among the first to bring tattooing into contemporary art, exhibiting at the Venice Biennale.

Education

My seminars go beyond simple technique — they transmit a structured method, a clear artistic vision, and solid professional discipline. Immersive, targeted experiences designed to form competent, conscious tattoo professionals.

Duration: 3 Days · Foundations of Tattooing
Day 1 — Theory

History & cultural significance, hygiene & safety standards, materials & tools, drawing theory, stencil creation, professional ethics.

Day 2 — Technique & Practice

Machine setup & demonstration, guided exercises on synthetic skin — hatching, line control, fill techniques — with individual feedback from Gabriele.

Day 3 — Live Practice

Real-simulation tattoo session on synthetic skin. Step-by-step supervision, collective review, individual feedback, and certificate presentation.
