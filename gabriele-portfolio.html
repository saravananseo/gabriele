How It Works

From vision
to skin.

  1. The Consultation

    Every great tattoo begins with a conversation. We explore your vision, your story, and the symbolism that matters to you — to build a concept that is entirely yours.

  2. The Design

    Gabriele crafts a bespoke design using classical drawing techniques, reference studies, and compositional mastery. You receive a detailed sketch before any ink touches skin.

  3. The Session

    In a calm, private studio environment, Gabriele brings the design to life with extraordinary precision. Sessions are paced to ensure both comfort and flawless execution.

  4. The Aftercare

    Detailed aftercare guidance ensures your tattoo heals to its full glory. A follow-up touch-up session is included for all major works.
